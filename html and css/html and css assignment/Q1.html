Early life

  • S. Nambi Narayanan was born into a Tamil family
  • Dr. Nambi's Malayalam autobiography was inaugrated by Kerala Trivandrum Parliament Member Dr.Shashi Tharoor.

Career

  • He is an Indian aerospace engineer who worked for the Indian Space Research Organisation.
  • He was the scientist was instrumental in developing the Vikas engine that would be used for the first PSLV that India launched.

Defameing

  • In 1994, he was falsely charged with espionage and arrested.
  • The charges against him were dismissed by the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) in April 1996, and the Supreme Court of India declared him not guilty in 1998.
